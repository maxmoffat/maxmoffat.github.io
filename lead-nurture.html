Lead Nurture Email Campaign - Overview

The focus for this email campaign was the multi-family industry (apartments, townhomes, etc.) and consumers timeline before renting a living space.

The objective was to design a variety of email templates, write the copy of the email to be dynamic for a client to customize the language, code the templates and then determine the timeline and workflow in which the emails would be sent to the consumer.

Research

Before sketching and beginning the design of the email templates, our team needed an understanding of what consumers would expect out of email correspondence and common design trends in email right now.

This took shape in evaluating a number of client sites to understand the content that is presented to their consumers. We then worked with clients to understand what objectives they look to have their consumers complete first and prioritize our content.

All of the emails we created were aimed at 3 target content focuses:

  • Educational - Inform the consumer about the buying process, what to know moving forward and things like this. This helps build a trust and connection with the consumer and property.
  • Promotional - Give the consumer insight to an opportunity the do not want to miss out on.
  • Interactive - Allows the consumer to "gain" something from completing whatever call-to-action is placed in the email.
